    
-------Youtube Music-------
/play - Play a song from YouTube.
/pause - Pause the current song.
/skip - Skip the current song.
/resume - Resume the paused song.
/stop - Stop the music and clear the queue.
/queue - List the song queue.
/nowplaying - Show the now playing song.
/loop - Loop the current playlist
/volume - Set Radio volume.
/disconnect - Disconnect from voice channel
-------Radio Commands-------
/jpop - Play listen.moe radio (J-Pop channel)
/kpop - Play listen.moe radio (K-Pop channel)
/qt - Play qtradio.moe radio
/qtnp - qtradio.moe now playing
/radioplay - Play your own radio from it's url
/disconnect - Disconnect from voice channel
